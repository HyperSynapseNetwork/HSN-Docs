# HSN Group Chat Rules

> To maintain a pleasant atmosphere in the group and ensure the safety and experience of all members, please read and follow the following group rules carefully. Joining the group indicates agreement to these rules.

## I. Identity and Safety

1. **Identity Verification Principle**: All online acquaintances claiming to be female will be treated as male-to-female crossplayers unless they proactively disclose their gender information from their ID card for verification. Please stay vigilant and guard against online fraud.
2. **Prohibition of Illegal Activities**: Any form of doxxing, cyberstalking, privacy trafficking, dissemination of obscene materials, or other illegal activities are strictly prohibited. Offenders will be immediately removed from the group; if serious consequences result, the management team reserves the right to report to the police and pursue legal liability according to law.
3. **No Reporting Group Members or Bots**: Please do not maliciously report other group members or HSNBot (except those disseminating obscene content).

## II. Speech and Content Standards

1. **No Sensitive Topics**: Discussions of religious or political content are not permitted in this group, nor may related links, videos, articles, or other media be sent. Historical figure-related images/videos that can be normally published on mainstream platforms such as Bilibili are exempt from this restriction.
2. **No Political Stance**: This group has no political stance or affiliation. It is forbidden to express any political views in the name of the group.
3. **Black/Grey Market Promotion Control**: Unauthorized promotion of black/grey market activities (including but not limited to ghostwriting papers, gambling site referrals, etc.) will result in: first offense — warning, second offense — one-day mute, third offense — permanent removal from the group.
4. **Content Moderation**:
   - No gambling or drugs; resolutely resist all drug and gambling-related content;
   - Mature content should be moderated. Excessively explicit content must be moved to the group album within 30 seconds. Failure to do so will result in a mute starting from one hour;
   - Real-life explicit content will result in an immediate mute with no grace period.

## III. Communication Order

1. **Conflict Resolution**: When disputes arise in the group, both parties should remain calm and communicate rationally, or contact an admin promptly for mediation. Personal attacks, flaming, and arguments are prohibited.
2. **Anti-Spam**: Please speak in moderation and avoid high-frequency spamming that affects others' communication. Those who spam too quickly may be randomly muted.

## IV. Supplementary Provisions

- These group rules take effect from the date of publication. The management team has the right to revise and supplement the rules according to actual circumstances;
- Those who object to the enforcement of the group rules may privately message an admin to provide feedback. Publicly questioning management decisions within the group is prohibited;
- Thank you for your cooperation and support. We wish everyone a pleasant experience.
